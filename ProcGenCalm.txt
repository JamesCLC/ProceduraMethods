Pause, Process, Plan, Prioritise, Proceed.

1) Pause - What's the issue?

2) Process - What are the likely causes?

3) Plan - How can I go about trying to fix this?

4) Prioritise - Which approach should I try next?

5) Proceed - Go until the list is exhausted, I am exhausted or the problem is fixed.
